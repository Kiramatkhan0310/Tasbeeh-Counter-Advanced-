Islamic Tasbih Counter

Tap the counter for Subhanallah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar and Kalma sounds. The counter is styled with a modern Islamic theme and works beautifully on mobile.

How to use Press the large button and the image will update with each dhikr phase. Sound plays automatically when audio is available.
Reset anytime Use restart to return to zero and start a fresh Tasbih cycle.
Sound control Toggle sound on or off while the counter is active.
